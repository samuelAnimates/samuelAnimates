SNOW QUEEN TRAVEL COMPANY

2013, USA

Animation (2D digital), live action video.

08min 14s.

Synopsis


Happiness is in the journey.

Travel to the north, following a map made of broken glass. Based on the short story "Travels with the Snow Queen" by Kelly Link, and the original Hans Christian Andersen fairy tale.

Recognition


Chicago REEL Shorts Film Festival, official selection, Chicago, IL, 2013.

Process

This film taught me a lot about the importance of seeking out feedback early and often, as well as the value not trying to squeeze a square peg into a circle. This film contains a lot of moments I very proud of—in fact I still say that this film's introduction is the best thing I've made. As my first major undertaking made as something more than university project, this film also holds a special place in my heart.


However, I'm also proud to say that I would follow a completely different process if I were aking this film today. I was inspired to make this film while attending the Stuttgart Festival of Animated Film. I felt the undeniable spark to make the kind of work I had never seen before, and had the idea for the walking scene of this film, inspired by Kelly Link's writing.
