SNOW QUEEN TRAVEL COMPANY

2013, USA

Animation (2D digital), live action video.

08min 14s.

Synopsis


Happiness is in the journey: Travel to the north, following a map made of broken glass. Based on the short story "Travels with the Snow Queen" by Kelly Link, and the original Hans Christian Andersen fairy tale.

Recognition

  • Chicago REEL Shorts Film Festival, official selection, Chicago, IL, 2013.

Process

If I were to remake this film, I would use a much more iterative process. Though there are a lot of moments I'm proud of in this film, I limited it as a whole by not allowing room for discovery and spontaneity in the filmmaking process. What started as a narrative short could've become a much more satisfying abstract film if I had let it.


I limited myself by thinking that I had to chart out a path and follow it precisely to successfully finish making a short film. Now, I know that I can follow an iterative process of test sequences, feedback, editing, and background research to allow mroe breathing room for lessons I might learn along the way toward completing a film.
