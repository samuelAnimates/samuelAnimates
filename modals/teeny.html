2016, USA

Kickstarter campaign video.

01min 03s.

Overview

Kickstarter campaign video for a handmade coinpurse by a Taiwan-based artisan.

Role

Director, editor, animator.

Goal

Use existing narration and product photos as a starting point to create a 1-minute promo video for a Kickstarter campaign.


My Role

I storyboarded a campaign video, wrote a shorter version of the existing narration for re-recording, created animation segments, shot new video footage of the product in use, edited existing photos, and edited the final video for use in the Kickstarter campaign.


Results

A short video serving as the campaign promo video for a successfully funded Kickstart project.
