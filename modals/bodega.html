PERSEFONE'S BREAKFAST

2015, USA/CHINA

Animation (2D digital, 3D digital, object), archival footage, live action video.

06min 44s.

Synopsis

"We're talking, and you're listening." Persephone leaves a series of voice messages for Hades as she prepares to leave the Underworld — once and for all. This experimental animation uses mythology + food to explore issues of identity and inequity in today's cities, as Persephone takes control of her fate in a world that was never designed for her.

Recognition

Chicagoland Shorts Vol. 3, Finalist, 2017.

Official Latino Short Film Festival, Exceptional Artistry award nomination, New York, NY, 2016.
