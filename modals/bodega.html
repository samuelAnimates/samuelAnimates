A BODEGA

2014, USA

Animation (stop-motion, pixilation), live action video.

05min 58s.

Synopsis:

An animated documentary made with ingredients available at a bodega in Lawrence, MA.

Recognition:

  • Chicago Onscreen: Local Film Showcase, official selection, Chicago Park District, 2017.
  • Chicago REEL Shorts Film Festival, Best Documentary nomination, Chicago, IL, 2014.
  • Blue Plum Animation Festival, official selection, Johnson City, TN, 2014.

Questions I Set Out to Answer:

  • What would it look like to highlight the existing assets in food deserts, instead of just focusing on the disparity?
  • What's the effect of combining stop-motion animation with traditional documentary film? Especially in discussions of social justice issues?
