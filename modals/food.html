ANIMATING FOOD

2013, USA

Animation, physical interactions with fruit

2014, USA

Animation, time-lapse zoom of fruit

2014, USA

Animation, silent food

Key Finding


Forward animation (animating one frame at a time, without following a storyboard) is a surprisingly similar process to documentary video, and experiments with this technique can help form new approaches to filming documentary subjects.

Context


These animations were made over the course of several months on the way to my film [A Bodega].

Implications for Future Work

  • A small-scale experiment can lead to new methods/approaches that can later be incorporated into a larger-scale project.

Process

These shorts arose out of spontaneity, animated under the camera with no plan in mind and no underlying objective. I simply bought the ingredients and set out to see what different qualitiesof the food I could bring out. Shot in one spontaneous burst, this lack of structure led to shots I would have never planned ahead of time, and techniques of object animation that would end up in [A Bodega].
