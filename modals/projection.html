PROJECTION MAPPING

2013, USA

Model test for a proposed art installation.

Partnership with Jackie Woonhyun Bae

2013, USA

Live action video, experimental animation.

04min 16s.

Overview

A method using projection, video, + animation to change the face of surfaces in the real world.

Context


Projects developed under the guidance of Allen Sayegh and Krzysztof Wodiczko during my time at Harvard University.

Process:

A 3D digital model was made of the surface to be projected upon. A video file was produced and virtually projected onto the 3D digital model. Views of that virtual projection were rendered from the persepctive of the projector. And finally, that rendering was projected onto the actual surface that was originally modeled, with the physical projector matching the settings of the virtual one..

Implications for Future Work:

  • This technique can guide people through experiences in real-world environments.
  • This technique can create interventions in spaces that encourage re-examination of everyday environments.
