AFTER, AFTER THAT

2012, USA

Live action video.

04min 16s.

Synopsis

An experimental animted documentary, a cross between two marks.

Context

Made for Alfred Guzzetti's class,
VES 52R, Introduction to Nonfiction Videomaking
Fall 2012, Harvard College

Process

I appreciate how animation methods emphasize motion and design over speech/text, and I wanted to leverage this to highlight the ways that experimental animation can document life stories where spoken language falls short. I chose scars + tattoos as my focus, because of their own visual language that typically serves as a shrothand for life stories.
