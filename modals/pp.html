PERCEPTIONS OF PLANNED PARENTHOOD

Overview

  • Qualitative semi-structured interview study around generalist medical providers' perceptions of Planned Parenthood with respect to their family planning referrals.
  • Independent follow-up investigation using the Media Cloud tool to examine framing of Planned Parenthood in mainstream media and conservative/liberal blogs since the US election.

Status

  • I am serving as first author of a manuscript of the qualitative study being submitted for publication.
  • I have created an initial [Media Cloud topic] and am looking into the initial results to see how I might refine my search and present interesting themes.
