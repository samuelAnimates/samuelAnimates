HEALTHY ON THE BLOCK
(BODEGAS SALUDABLES)

2014, USA

Intro video for a local public health program in Lawrence, MA.

(Excerpt)

Overview


An introductory video for a public health program by the [ Mayor's Health Task Force], for use in meetings with potential stakeholders.

Context


Made for community partners at the [Viswanath Lab] while working on my senior thesis film [A Bodega].

Process

I actually started off the process of making [A Bodega] as a documentary about a public health program by the Lawrence Mayor's Health Task Force (MHTF). They were starting a great program focused on helping corner stores stock fresh/frozen prodce, as well host cooking demos and excerises classesâ€”all to create an environment of health around local resources. I visited bodegas and sat in on meetings with the MHTF, recording interviews and documentary footage along the way.


However, the timeline of the MHTF project ended up not aligning with my own timeline for finishing my thesis film and graduating from college. There was still a need for a tool to help explain the MHTF's goals + engage potential stakeholders. So, I worked on this video while finishing my thesis as a way to honor my commitments to our partnership.


Lessons Learned

Though I had contributed to the relathionships with community partners while working at the [Viswanath Lab], this my first time leading community partner engagement on a project. I felt honored by the MHTF and the Viswanath Lab members' eagerness to help me grow through this experience, trying to create a project that would satisfy my thesis committee, my lab members, the MHTF + their partners all at once.


This process didn't play out exactly as I had planned, and I ended up producing two products I am very proud of as a result.
