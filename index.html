ABOUT ME

SAMUEL R. MENDEZ

Filmmaker, researcher, digital creator.

I grew up in Chicago, studied animation & global health at Harvard, directed video projects internationally, and currently work on community-engaged public health research at Northwestern.
I want to develop video + stop-motion animation as participatory research techniques, and build platforms for community research partners to document and analyze their environments as they are related to social equity.
I prioritize flexibility, concrete outcomes, and listening when working on a project.

10 Days in Saigon

A 10-week web series documenting wheelchair travel in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
