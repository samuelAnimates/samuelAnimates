PERSONAL STATEMENT

I believe in the power of digital media to
catalyze social awareness.


I believe in the power of social awareness to
advance social equity.


I believe that when it comes to working toward this goal,
digital media alone are not enough.


So, I do my work across
animation, qualitative research, web development, + public art.

I create work that
guides people through real-world spaces in new ways.


I create work that bridges
digital media + physical action.


I create work that explores new spaces for
understanding others' lived experiences.


I aim to address longstanding problems by
asking new questions to arrive at new answers.

I want to weave these modes of communication together in order to
bypass knee-jerk reactions to information around social equity.


I learn the skills I need through
making the kind of work I want to see.
