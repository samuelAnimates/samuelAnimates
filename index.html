ABOUT ME

"I could trace about 80% of my humor back to specific SpongeBob episodes."

Interests: community-based research, social determinants of health, accessibility, digital documentary methods.

I make animated short films, I conduct community-based research, and I do front-end web development with an eye toward web accessibility. As I recognize the time & effort I put into learning these skills, I recognize the privilege of having such chances to do so in the first place. I want use the chances I've been given to make the spaces & systems I occupy accessible to a wider range of people.

Long-term Goals: develop animation and video as participatory research methods; build empathy across community research partners and increase accessibility of such partnerships; develop research partnership models and platforms to disseminate lessons learned.
