SOCIAL DETERMINANTS OF HEALTH:

the conditions in which we
live, learn, work + play;

the conditions that shape the
choices in our daily lives;

the conditions that, in turn,
shape our long-term health.

MY GOALS

I aim to use digital media
to create new spaces for

social change + community engagement.


I plan to achieve this
through a focus on

social determinants + underserved communities

I believe this requires the ability
to look at the world through

complementary lenses,


interpret it through

complementary methods,


show others what I've learned,

+ empower others to do the same.
